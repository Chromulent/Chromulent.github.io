Chromulent Homepage

“Chromulent” is a strange neologism. An awful attempt at combining the words ‘cromulent’ a word meaning “acceptable or legitimate” and ‘Chrono’ the Latin for time.

Why “Chromulent” ?

I want nothing more than to provide simplicity, remove confusion, and allow for repeatable tasks. This is done with a directory of files. That is all.

The purpose of "Chromulent" and hopefully all content within; is to simplify simple tasks and reduce stress.

Naming Schema

The Naming Scheme of Chromulent is as follows:

Primary-Secondary-Tertiary_Specific

  • Primary - The action or affect that we are manipluating on the second variable.
  • Secondary - What we are affecting which is indicitive of what the purpose for the file is.
  • Tertiary - What we are changing on the second variable.
  • Specific - If nedeed, a specific tag that will help us identify it from another like named file.
