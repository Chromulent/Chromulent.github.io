Chromulent Homepage

“Chromulent” is a strange neologism. An awful attempt at combining the words ‘cromulent’ a word meaning “acceptable or legitimate” and ‘Chrono’ the Latin for time.

Why “Chromulent” ?

I want nothing more than provide simplicity, to an immense complicated topic, such as IT and Scripting. This is done with a directory of files. That is all. Please read further down for more information.

The purpose of "Chromulent" and hopefully all content within; is to simplify simple tasks and reduce stress.
